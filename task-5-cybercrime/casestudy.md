# Cybercrime Case Study: UPI Fraud

UPI fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India today. It usually happens when scammers trick people into sharing sensitive details or approving fake payment requests.

A typical case starts when a fraudster calls or messages pretending to be from a bank, customer care, or even a known contact. They may send a fake link or ask the victim to scan a QR code. The victim is then asked to enter their UPI PIN or approve a payment request.

Most victims are students or people who are not fully aware of digital payment safety. Once the victim enters their PIN or approves the request, money is instantly transferred to the scammer’s account.

The consequences can be serious, including financial loss and stress. Sometimes it is difficult to recover the money.

This case shows the importance of awareness and being careful while using online payment platforms.
