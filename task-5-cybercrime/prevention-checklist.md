# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
- Do not click on unknown links or messages
- Always verify payment requests before approving
- Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown sources
- Use secure and trusted apps only
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Regularly check your bank statements
- Do not trust calls claiming to be from bank officials

## Reporting

- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
