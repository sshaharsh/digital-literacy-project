# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional profile
- Think before posting anything
- Use privacy settings properly
- Share useful and positive content
- Respect others’ opinions

## ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t share personal/sensitive information
- Don’t engage in online arguments
- Don’t post offensive content
- Don’t trust unknown links
- Don’t overshare your daily life
